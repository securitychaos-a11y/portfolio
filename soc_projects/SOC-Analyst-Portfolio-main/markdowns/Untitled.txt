
# Mission 0.4 – Verify Sysmon

## Objective

Verify that Sysmon is actively monitoring endpoint activity.

## Verification Steps

- Confirmed Sysmon service is running.
- Opened Sysmon Operational log.
- Generated a test event by launching Notepad.
- Verified that Sysmon recorded the event.

## Observation

Sysmon successfully captured the creation of a new process, confirming that endpoint telemetry is being generated.
